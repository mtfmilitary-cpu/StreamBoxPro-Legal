Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Welcome to StreamBox Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web portal and Windows media player application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our syncing and verification services, we collect limited information when you interact with our platform:

  • Account Profile Data: When you log in via Google Firebase, we receive your basic profile information including your display name, email address, and profile picture URL.
  • Application Data: We store custom streaming configurations and media link arrays that you choose to sync to our cloud servers.
  • Subscription Status: If you connect your Patreon account, we receive subscription validation data (active/inactive status) to unlock premium features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and securely manage your cloud profile.
  • Synchronize your custom media links between your web dashboard and the StreamBox Pro Windows application.
  • Verify premium status tiers through the Patreon API.

3. Third-Party Integrations

Our application relies on secure, industry-standard third-party services to function. These services handle your data in accordance with their own privacy guidelines:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure user authentication and database storage (Cloud Firestore).
  • Netlify: Hosts our secure serverless backend functions which process API requests.
  • Patreon Developer Portal: Handles billing authentication and subscription checks securely without passing payment details to us.

4. Data Security & Storage

Your synced data is protected inside private, encrypted Firestore databases. We do not sell, trade, rent, or distribute your private links or account information to any third parties. Your configuration files are completely private to your authenticated account.

5. Your Data Rights

You can view, edit, or clear your synced audio links directly from your web portal at any time. If you wish to permanently delete your account profile and all cloud-stored configurations, please contact us directly at your support email.
